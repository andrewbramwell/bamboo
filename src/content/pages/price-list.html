Discover our Signature Massage Treatments

Our skilled therapist is committed to helping you relax, rejuvenate, and find relief from stress and tension. Whether you seek targeted relief or a pampering experience, we have the perfect massage for you.

    30 Minutes £30

    This ancient technique combines acupressure, Indian Ayurvedic principles, and assisted yoga postures. Thai Massage doesn't use oils, instead, the therapist uses hands, knees, legs, and feet to move you into yoga-like stretches, improving flexibility, reducing tension, and enhancing energy flow.

    90 Minutes £75


    This soothing treatment combines gentle massage techniques with essential oils tailored to your needs. It's excellent for stress relief and emotional balance.   • Swedish Massage

    30 Minutes £30

    60 Minutes £45

    90 Minutes £75


    This classic massage uses long, flowing strokes, kneading, and circular movements on superficial layers of muscle. It's excellent for relaxation, stress relief, and improving circulation. Swedish massage can help ease muscle tension, improve flexibility, and create a sense of overall well-being.

  • Foot Massage

    30 Minutes £30


    This specialised treatment involves systematic pressure application to specific points on the feet and lower legs. It's excellent for foot aches and general well-being. Based on the principle that foot points correspond to body organs, this massage aims to release blockages and promote overall health.

  • Head Massage

    30 Minutes £30


    This rejuvenating treatment focuses on the head, neck, and shoulders. It's ideal for relieving tension headaches and promoting relaxation. Using various pressure and movement techniques, it improves circulation to the head, reduces stress, and can even help improve hair and scalp condition.
