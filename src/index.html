Deep Tissue Thai Massage

Deep tissue massage targets chronic tension in muscles far below the body's surface. Using slow strokes and firm pressure, this technique aims to break down adhesions - bands of rigid tissue that can cause pain and restrict movement. Our skilled therapist applies pressure with fingers, thumbs, and occasionally elbows, focusing on areas of persistent tension such as stiff necks, tight lower backs, and sore shoulders.

Unlike relaxation massages, deep tissue massage addresses specific problems. It's particularly effective for chronic pain, limited mobility, injury recovery, and conditions like fibromyalgia or osteoarthritis. At Bamboo Thai Massage we tailor this powerful technique to your individual needs, working to alleviate pain, improve mobility, and restore your body's natural balance.

Featured Treatment

Thai Massage

Thai massage – also known as Thai Yoga Massage, combines assisted yoga with acupressure techniques. The therapist will incorporate yogic stretching movements as they knead and press muscles along the body’s energy lines with the hands and feet. Unlike traditional Western massage techniques, Thai massage doesn't use oils or lotions.

This ancient art is both deeply relaxing and energising. It's particularly effective for improving flexibility, reducing muscle tension and spasms, and enhancing athletic performance. Thai massage can also help relieve various types of pain and even headaches. At Bamboo Thai Massage our experienced therapist Jan brings techniques directly from Thailand, offering you an authentic massage experience tailored to your body's needs.
